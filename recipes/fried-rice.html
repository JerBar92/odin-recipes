Fried Rice

A quick recipe like what you get in your favorite Chinese restaurant

Ingredients

  • ⅔ cup chopped baby carrots
  • ½ cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced, or to taste (Optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil, or to taste

Directions

  1. Assemble ingredients.
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and
    cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and
    garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs
    with vegetables.
  4. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil
    and toss again.
