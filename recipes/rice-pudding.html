Rice Pudding

This creamiest rice pudding will remember you the one of your childhood

Ingredients

  • ½ gallon milk
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice
  • 3 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste

Directions

  1. Combine 1/2 gallon milk, sugar, and rice in a large saucepan over medium-low
    heat. Simmer, covered, for 1 hour, stirring frequently. Remove the pan from heat
    and let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Whisk together eggs, 1/4 cup milk, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Stir into rice mixture and return the pan to low heat, stirring constantly, for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour pudding into a 9x13-inch dish and cover with plastic wrap, folding back the
    corners to allow steam to escape. Allow pudding to cool to room temperature.
  5. Remove plastic wrap and sprinkle the surface of pudding with cinnamon. Cover
    tightly with fresh plastic wrap and refrigerate for 8 hours to overnight before serving.
